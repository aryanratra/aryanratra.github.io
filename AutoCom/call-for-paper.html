Call For Paper

International Conference on Automation & Computation

AutoCom 2022 invites original contributions based on the results of research and developments. Prospective authors are requested to submit their papers in not more than 6 pages, as PDF prepared in the two column IEEE format. All the accepted and presented papers will be eligible for submission as e-proceedings in IEEE Xplore Which indexed with world's leading Abstracting & Indexing (A&I) databases, including ISI / SCOPUS/ Google Scholar.

Submissions for ICACITE conference are welcomed in the following areas:

  • Big Data Analytics

  • Machine learning

  • Data Visualizations

  • Medicine and Healthcare

  • Data Fusion

  • Bio Informatics

  • Green Communications

  • Networks, IoT and Cyber Security

  • Cluster, Cloud, & Grid Computing

  • Neural Networks and Deep Learning

  • Image / Video / Multimedia Signal Processing

  • Emerging memory technologies

  • Power Generation, Transmission and Distribution

  • Power System Monitoring, Control and Protection

  • Micro Grids& Distributed Generation

  • Control Communication and Monitoring of Smart Grid

  • Space and underwater robots

  • Control system modeling

  • Intelligent control, Neuro-control, Fuzzy control and their applications

  • Adaptive Control and Non Linear Control and their applications

  • Fashion Technology

  • Industrial Automation

  • Optimization Techniques

  • Artificial Intelligence

  • Mechtronics

  • Robotics and Automation

  • Wireless Communications

  • Network Communication

  • Multimedia Communication

  • Wireless Sensor Networks

  • Micro Electro Mechanical systems (MEMS)

  • Power System Modeling, Simulation and Analysis

  • Renewable Energy Sources and Applications


Papers from other topics relevant to conference theme or the special sessions are also welcome. Submission of papers shall be through.
