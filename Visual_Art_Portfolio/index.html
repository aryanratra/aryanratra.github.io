About Me

A brief history

Ever since I was a kid, I wanted to make stuff. I wasn't much interested in playing outdoor sports and often found myself making cool things like LEGO Buildings, artwork on MSWLogo and even short films using my father's old camera and my brother as the actor. As I grew up, this hunger of mine for creating new things grew exponentially and with that grew new opportunities.

I spent most of my time in high school making films. I was a part of the Tech Board and would often represent my school in inter-school filmmaking and photography competitions. Being a member of the Tech Board also enabled me to be highly technical as we used to manage all the hardware including lights and sound systems during various school programmes.

In college, I got lots of exposure to the field of computer science and made some awesome mini-projects including some basic websites, encryption-decryption programmes and an Enigma Machine Simulator (currently being worked on). I have learnt a lot during this journey and hope that I continue to learn new stuff each and every day...
